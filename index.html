Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the American Airlines helpline provide?

Our helpline assists with booking modifications, cancellations, onboard services, emergency contacts, special needs accommodations, and general Airline information.

Can I get help with my existing reservation?

Yes, our representatives can assist with all aspects of your existing reservation including room upgrades, dining preferences, excursion bookings, and special requests.

What information should I have ready when I call?

Please have your reservation number, sailing date, ship name, and any relevant details about your inquiry ready to help us serve you more efficiently.
